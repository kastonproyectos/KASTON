How It Works

K-LOCK System

"Like a LEGO, but indestructible."

What makes KASTON unique is its connection engineering. Forget loose tiles that blow away in the wind.

  • Male/Female System Each tile has a lateral tab (male) and a receiving groove (female). When joined, they become mechanically locked together.
  • Wind Resistance Thanks to the interlocking and two anchor points to the mesh, we eliminate the risk of detachment from strong winds.

K-LOCK Specification

Each tile weighs only 300 g but withstands loads of 4,200–5,500 PSI. The dual interlocking + mechanical anchor system turns the roof into a monolithic surface of 37 interconnected pieces/m².

Indestructible

  • Total Armor — 5,500 PSI Breaking load from 4,200 to 5,500 PSI. Hail? Walking on the roof? Impacts? They absorb the blow without breaking. Shore D hardness 68–72. Zero waste, zero replacements.
  • Insulation — Thermal Range -15°C to 90°C Unlike zinc that turns your home into an oven, our dense polymer insulates temperature and noise. Operates without degradation from -15°C to 90°C.
  • Lightweight — 300 g per Tile Only 11.1 kg/m² (vs. clay: 50 kg/m²). Reduces wood or metal structure costs by up to 40%.
